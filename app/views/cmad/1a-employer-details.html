Things you need to know

Your employer is a flexi-job apprenticeship agency and they will pay your wages.

Flexi-job apprenticeship agencies are organisations who employ apprentices and arrange placements with one or more host employers.

You may have several placements during your apprenticeship to learn the skills you need to complete your apprenticeship training.

Before starting your apprenticeship your flexi-job apprenticeship agency should have given you a:

  • copy of your apprenticeship agreement signed by you and them, if you have not received this agreement please speak to your flexi-job apprenticeship agency
  • day-to-day contact or manager who will support you through your apprenticeship
