Things you need to know about a flexi-job agency apprenticeship

During your apprenticeship, you may move between different placements found by your flexi-job apprenticeship agency.

Your planned training start and estimated duration should:

  • include the time you will spend in off-the-job training across all placements
  • not include when your end point assessment will take place
The estimated duration of your apprenticeship may change during your apprenticeship after discussions with your flexi-job apprenticeship agency and your training provider.
